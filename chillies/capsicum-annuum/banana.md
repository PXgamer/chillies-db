# Banana

## Description

> A sweet pepper with low heat that is usually picked when bright yellow or green. They have long cylindrical fruits that taper to a point. If left, they will ripen through orange and red. 

## General Information

Key | Value
--- | ---
Species | [Capsicum Annuum](.)
Origin | USA
Scoville (SHU) | ~0 - 500
Heat Level | Mild

## Growing

Key | Value
--- | -----
Hardiness | USDA Zone 9
Spread | 40cm high (centimetres)
Best suited for | Outdoors and patios
Lighting | Full sun
Time to fruit | ~75 days

## Notes, hints and tips

> Great for frying or pickling, and they are also excellent in sandwiches.
